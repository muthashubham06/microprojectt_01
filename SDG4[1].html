Why Quality Education Matters

Education creates skilled thinkers, prepares people for jobs, and helps societies solve major challenges.

Education reduces poverty

More schooling increases income and breaks poverty cycles.

Education improves health

Literate populations make healthier life decisions. Educating girls improves family well-being.

Principles of Quality Education

  • Inclusive & equal access
  • Safe learning environments
  • Relevant, skill-based content
